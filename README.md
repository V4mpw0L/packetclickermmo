## 🎮 **Core Game Features**

### 🖱️ **Advanced Clicking System**
- **Responsive Click Mechanics** with zero-latency sound feedback
- **Dynamic Combo System** with 5 progressive tiers:
  - 🟢 **GREEN** (1-4 clicks) - Basic clicking
  - 🟡 **GOLD** (5-14 clicks) - Entry combo bonus
  - 🔵 **MEGA** (15-49 clicks) - Medium challenge with visual effects
  - 🟣 **ULTRA** (50-199 clicks) - Hard achievement with epic bonuses
  - 🔴 **ANIMAL** (200+ clicks) - **LEGENDARY** tier requiring 35+ seconds of sustained clicking!
- **Critical Hit System** with customizable multipliers
- **Visual Cursor Changes** that reflect your current combo tier

### 📦 **Revolutionary Equipment System**
- **100 Unique Items** across 6 rarity tiers with beautiful sprite artwork
- **4 Equipment Slots** in a compact 2x2 grid layout for optimal space usage
- **Smart Equipment UI** with centered item display, stats, and one-click equipping
- **Balanced Item Economy** with doubled selling rewards:
  - 🟢 **Common**: 50 packets
  - 🟡 **Rare**: 200 packets  
  - 🔵 **Epic**: 1,000 packets
  - 🟣 **Ultra**: 5,000 packets
  - 🔴 **Animal**: 20,000 packets

### ⭐ **Deep Progression Systems**

#### **Prestige System** (Unlock at 50,000 packets)
- **Auto Clicker** - Automated clicking up to 10 levels
- **Packet Boost** - 10% packet multiplier per level (20 levels max)
- **Gem Find** - 5% chance to find gems while clicking (5 levels)
- **Crit Boost** - Increased critical hit chance (15 levels)
- **Offline Earnings** - Earn packets while away (24 levels = 24 hours)
- **Lucky Clicks** - 1% chance for 10x packet rewards (10 levels)
- **Mega Crits** - Upgrade critical multiplier beyond 2x (5 levels)
- **Gem Magnet** - Generate gems from idle packets (8 levels)

#### **Temporary Boost Shop**
Power up with gems for immediate advantages:
- **🔥 Double Packets** (5 minutes) - 3 gems
- **💎 Triple Gems** (10 minutes) - 8 gems  
- **⚡ Quad Click Power** (3 minutes) - 5 gems
- **✨ Mega Crit Mode** (2 minutes) - 12 gems
- **🤖 Auto-Clicker** (1 minute) - 15 gems
